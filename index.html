Michael J. Hacia, CPA

A California Certified Public Accountant since 1992

Licensed in California & Nevada

Before starting my career in tax, I served seven years in the California Air National Guard and saw active duty in the Persian Gulf with the USAF during Operation Desert Shield and Operation Desert Storm.

Born and raised in the San Fernando Valley, California, I received my bachelor's degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Accounting from California State University, Northridge. I continued my education at Golden Gate University in San Francisco where I received my master's degree in Taxation.

In 2008, I was the tax partner of a large privately-held business management firm in Los Angeles where I specialized in complex tax concerns encompassing clients in the entertainment industry and small-to-mid-market business enterprises.
